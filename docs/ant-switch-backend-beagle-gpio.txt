This GPIO backend to KiwiSDR antenna switch extension

http://wiki.seeed.cc/BeagleBone_Green/

This extension uses Beagle GPIO pins

P8 pin 11 - Antenna 1
P8 pin 12 - Antenna 2
P8 pin 13 - Antenna 3
P8 pin 14 - Antenna 4
P8 pin 15 - Antenna 5
P8 pin 16 - Antenna 6
P8 pin 17 - Antenna 7
P8 pin 18 - Antenna 8
P8 pin 19 - Antenna 9
P8 pin 26 - Antenna 10
